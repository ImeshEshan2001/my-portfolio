About Me

With over six years of hands-on experience, I work across studio production, live sound, and session performance. I founded Musix Studios in 2019, a professional recording facility in Sri Lanka.

My work covers the full audio process, from recording and vocal production to mixing, mastering, and live sound system setup. Alongside engineering, I actively perform as a session and live musician, collaborating closely with music directors to deliver clean, musical results.
